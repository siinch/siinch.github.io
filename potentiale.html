ABOUT POTENTIALE

Potentiale is a classic platformer filled with atmosphere and motivational quotes, to help guide the player through their journey. It was made with the Unity game engine.

Potentiale started as a school project, to see if a game could be designed to give people a more positive outlook on life. It was made in 2015 and is the first game I ever finished and released.

The experiments showed that Potentiale did not have any significant effect on the positivity of the testers. However, it has managed to remind me of my passion for game development many times.
